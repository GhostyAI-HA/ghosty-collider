## üëª VISION: The Pilgrimage Loop

> "A game that is *designed* to be left ‚Äî and designs the leaving as part of the experience. An AI companion tends to your world while you're away, and when you return, it shows you what grew in your absence."

**Feel:** Wonder, nostalgia, gentle surprise

**Image:** A player opens the app after 2 weeks away. Their AI companion says, "While you were gone, the garden bloomed. A traveler visited ‚Äî they left you a letter." The world didn't freeze. It lived.

**Why Now:** AI agents can now autonomously tend to game worlds. Before 2025, NPCs could only run scripted events. Now they can make contextual decisions, making "off-screen life" genuinely surprising.
